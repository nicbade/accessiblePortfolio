About Nic Bade Get to know Nic

After working for 24 years in Food Service I decided I'd made enough Bloody Mary's and Pancakes for my lifetime. This led me to Prime Digital Academy to pursue a Certificate for Full Stack Software Development Engineering. A mouthful I know!

My end goal is simple. I want to create a space for city youth to learn how to code.

In my free time I coach, play and watch hockey. Enjoy spending time with my wife, Emily and dog Chloe. Also, enjoy good beer, coversations and spending time with good people.
