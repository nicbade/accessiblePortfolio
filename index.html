About Nic Bade Get to know Nic

I formerly worked with food service and with youth for 20+ years. Worked at restaurants such as Ward 6, Doolittle's Air Cafe, TGIFriday's, Caribou Coffee to name a few.

In June of 2017 I left the restaurant industry to pursue a career as a Software Engineer. I graduated from Prime Digital Academy in October 2017. My long term goal is to create a non-profit to teach youth in an urban environment how to code.

In my free time I coach, play and watch hockey. Enjoy spending time with my wife, Emily and dog Chloe. Also, enjoy good beer, coversations and spending time with good people.
